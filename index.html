Interesting Facts About the Camel

  • Camels can store water in their humps and go up to 7 months without water!
  • Camels cannot survive in any wet area. They have to be in a dry area, where they can naturally find the resources they need.
  • Camels have three sets of eyelids and two rows of eyelashes to keep sand out of their eyes.

Interesting Facts about the Panda

  • Giant pandas are good at climbing trees and can also swim!
  • Pandas go from pink to white and black! They come in all different colors!
  • Pandas can eat 12-38 kilograms of bamboo a day!

Interesting Facts about the Chameleon

  • Chameleons have eyes in the backs of their heads!
  • Chameleons have 360 vision eyes, meaning they can see behind, to both sides, and forward with no struggle at all! They dont even have to move their necks!
  • Chameleons can camoflauge into any background, meaning they can change the color of their skin!
